Welcome!

Here is the concept of the website.
The basic problem is that you can not travel as soon as you have a great travel idea. You need a way to collect them.
This website heps you to collect your ideas with a fun addition!
You can upload your travel ideas in the Upload menu.
When uploading, you have to give it a name, a brief description (you can add POIs or information about transportation)
and you have to enter the time you need for your trip.
When you have some free time for traveling but you do not know where to go you can simply visit the website.
Under Get Random menu you have to enter the length of your free time
and the site will show your next trip randomly selected from your previously uploaded ideas.
